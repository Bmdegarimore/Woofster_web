About Woofster


Woofster is dedicated to helping pet parents improve the health and wellness of their canine companions. Our goal is to provide a one stop shop for all of your pet's needs. We have developed an online and mobile platform that connects dog owners with pet service providers and pet friendly establishments in their area and incorporates the ability to track medication and learn more about their pet's health needs. We believe that the bond between a dog and their owner is unlike any other bond and as such should be nurtured.


The Team


We are a passionate team of software development and marketing professionals who feel that understanding our pet's needs is critical to ensuring that they live a happy healthy life. Our expertise in pets, technology, and marketing has driven us to develop a platform to connect other pet parents to all the resources that they need for their canine companion in one easy to use location.
